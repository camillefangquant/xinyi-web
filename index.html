About Me

I am a Blockchain Developer, Data Scientist, and Risk Management Specialist with a focus on building resilient financial systems. Since 2018, I have been bridging the gap between sophisticated data analytics and the evolving world of decentralized finance.

With an MBA from CEIBS Shanghai (2022) and a B.Sc in Finance, my approach to the markets is guided by a core philosophy: "Protecting against loss is more important than seeking profit." While I build advanced trading models, my priority is always the integrity of the framework and the robustness of the risk management strategy.

I believe that in the world of high-frequency data and volatile assets, the winner isn't just the one with the best algorithm, but the one who survives the stress tests.
